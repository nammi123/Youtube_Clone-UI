While French fries may be undeniably delicious, it's important to note that they are not typically associated with significant health benefits. French fries are typically deep-fried in oil, which increases their calorie and fat content. However, it's worth mentioning that potatoes themselves offer some nutritional value. They are a good source of dietary fiber, vitamin C, potassium, and certain antioxidants.To make French fries a bit healthier, consider alternative cooking methods such as baking or air-frying instead of deep-frying. Additionally, opting for sweet potato fries can provide additional nutrients like beta-carotene.
